# EU Standard Contractual Clauses (2021)

Reference for Module Two: Controller to Processor transfers under Commission Implementing Decision (EU) 2021/914.

---

## Overview

The 2021 EU SCCs replaced the previous (2010) SCCs and must be used for new contracts from 27 September 2021. Existing contracts using old SCCs must have been updated by 27 December 2022.

### Modules

| Module | Transfer Type | Use When |
|--------|--------------|----------|
| Module 1 | Controller → Controller | Two independent controllers |
| **Module 2** | Controller → Processor | **Most DPAs - controller engages processor** |
| Module 3 | Processor → Processor | Processor engages sub-processor |
| Module 4 | Processor → Controller | Rare - processor transfers to controller |

**For DPAs, use Module Two.**

---

## Module Two: Controller to Processor

### Section I: General Clauses

**Clause 1: Purpose and Scope**
- Ensure compliance with GDPR for transfers to third countries
- Apply when data importer not subject to GDPR

**Clause 2: Effect and Invariability**
- Clauses provide appropriate safeguards under Art. 46(1)
- Cannot be modified except to add parties or select options

**Clause 3: Third-Party Beneficiaries**
- Data subjects are third-party beneficiaries
- Can enforce Clauses 1-3, 8-9, 11-12, 14-16, 18(d)(e)

**Clause 4: Interpretation**
- Consistent with GDPR
- Terms have GDPR meanings

**Clause 5: Hierarchy**
- SCCs prevail over other agreements between parties on same subject

**Clause 6: Description of Transfer**
- Details in Annex I.B

**Clause 7: Docking Clause** *(Optional)*
```
[ ] INCLUDE - Allows other entities to accede to SCCs later
[ ] EXCLUDE
```

### Section II: Obligations of the Parties

**Clause 8: Data Protection Safeguards**

8.1 Instructions
- Data importer processes only on documented instructions
- Must inform exporter if legally unable to comply
- Must inform if instruction infringes Data Protection Law

8.2 Purpose Limitation
- Process only for purposes in Annex I.B

8.3 Transparency
- Make copy of SCCs available to data subjects on request

8.4 Security
- Implement technical and organizational measures in Annex II
- Grant access only to personnel on need-to-know basis
- Ensure personnel committed to confidentiality

8.5 Documentation and Compliance
- Deal promptly with exporter inquiries
- Submit to audits (with reasonable notice, during business hours)
- Make available information to demonstrate compliance

8.6 Use of Sub-processors
```
OPTION 1 - SPECIFIC PRIOR AUTHORIZATION
[ ] Data importer shall not sub-contract without exporter's prior specific authorization

OPTION 2 - GENERAL AUTHORIZATION
[ ] Data importer has general authorization for sub-processors listed in Annex III
    - Must inform of any intended changes with [SPECIFY TIME PERIOD] notice
    - Exporter may object within [SPECIFY TIME PERIOD]
```

Sub-processor requirements:
- Written contract with same data protection obligations
- Importer remains fully liable
- Provide copy of sub-processor contract to exporter on request

8.7 Data Subject Rights
- Deal with data subject requests promptly (within one month)
- Assist exporter in fulfilling data subject rights obligations
- If receiving direct request, inform data subject to submit to exporter

8.8 Personal Data Breach
- Notify exporter without undue delay of any breach
- Cooperate with exporter to address breach
- Provide information required for exporter to notify supervisory authority and data subjects

8.9 Non-compliance with the Clauses
- Promptly inform exporter if unable to comply
- Exporter entitled to suspend transfer until compliance or terminate

**Clause 9: Use of Sub-processors**
*(Complements Clause 8.6)*

9(a): Sub-processor requirements:
- Same contractual obligations as in SCCs
- Data importer remains fully liable
- Importer conducts regular compliance audits of sub-processor

9(b): If sub-processor fails obligations:
- Exporter may instruct importer to suspend/terminate sub-processing
- Exporter may terminate SCCs

9(c): Sub-processor located in third country:
- Must be subject to same SCCs (Module 3) or equivalent safeguards
- Prior authorization from exporter required for further sub-contracting

**Clause 10: Data Subject Rights** *(Module Two)*
- Parties assist each other in responding to data subject requests
- Importer deals with requests on behalf of exporter
- Applies to: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, objection
- Importer cannot charge for handling requests

**Clause 11: Redress** *(Optional)*
```
[ ] INCLUDE - Independent dispute resolution body
    Importer agrees that data subjects may lodge complaint with:
    [NAME OF BODY]
    At no cost to data subject

[ ] EXCLUDE
```

**Clause 12: Liability**
- Each party liable for damages caused by breach of SCCs
- Where both parties responsible, each liable for entire damage (joint and several)
- Entitled to claim back portion corresponding to other's responsibility

### Section III: Local Laws and Obligations

**Clause 13: Local Laws and Practices**
- Parties warrant no reason to believe destination country laws prevent compliance
- Declaration based on:
  - Specific circumstances of transfer
  - Laws and practices of destination country
  - Contractual, technical, organizational safeguards
- Importer warrants it has made best efforts to provide exporter with relevant information
- Exporter agrees to document assessment and make available to supervisory authority

**Clause 14: Obligations in Case of Government Access Requests**
14(a): Notification
- Notify exporter promptly of any legally binding request
- If prohibited from notifying, use best efforts to obtain waiver

14(b): Review of Legality
- Review legality of request under destination country law
- Challenge if reasonable grounds to consider unlawful
- Exhaust available remedies

14(c): Minimum Disclosure
- If required to disclose, disclose minimum amount necessary

14(d): Documentation
- Document requests received and responses
- Make available to exporter on request

**Clause 15: Obligations in Case of Direct Access by Public Authorities**
- If importer becomes aware of direct access:
  - Notify exporter immediately
  - Notify data subjects if possible
- Document and make available

### Section IV: Final Provisions

**Clause 16: Non-Compliance and Termination**
16(a): Infringement notification
- Importer must inform exporter of any infringement

16(b): Suspension
- Exporter may suspend transfer if:
  - Breach of SCCs
  - Importer unable to comply with SCCs
  - Court or authority decision in destination country
- Resume only when compliance assured or SCCs terminated

16(c): Termination
- Exporter entitled to terminate if compliance not restored within reasonable time

16(d): Post-termination
- Data to be returned or destroyed
- Importer continues to be bound by SCCs until data destroyed/returned

**Clause 17: Governing Law**
```
SCCs governed by law of EU Member State that allows third-party beneficiary rights.
Selected Member State: [SPECIFY - e.g., Ireland, Netherlands, Germany]
```

**Clause 18: Choice of Forum and Jurisdiction**
```
(a) Disputes between exporter and importer: courts of [SAME MEMBER STATE AS CLAUSE 17]
(b) Data subject may bring proceedings in:
    - Courts of Member State in (a), OR
    - Courts of Member State where data subject has habitual residence
(c) Parties accept jurisdiction of those courts
```

For UK GDPR transfers, append the UK IDTA to the EU SCCs.

## Implementation Checklist

When incorporating SCCs into a DPA:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Confirm Module Two applies (Controller → Processor) |
| 2 | Select optional clauses (Clause 7, 11) |
| 3 | Select sub-processor authorization (Clause 9 Option 1 or 2) |
| 4 | Specify governing law (Clause 17) |
| 5 | Specify jurisdiction (Clause 18) |
| 6 | Complete Annex I.A (Parties) |
| 7 | Complete Annex I.B (Description of transfer) |
| 8 | Complete Annex I.C (Supervisory authority) |
| 9 | Complete Annex II (Security measures) |
| 10 | Complete Annex III (Sub-processors) |
| 11 | Add UK IDTA if UK data transferred |
| 12 | Conduct Transfer Impact Assessment |
| 13 | Document supplementary measures if needed |
