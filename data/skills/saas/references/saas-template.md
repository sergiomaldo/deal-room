## SECTION 1: DEFINITIONS

**1.1** "Affiliate" means any entity that directly or indirectly controls, is controlled by, or is under common control with a Party, where "control" means ownership of more than 50% of the voting securities.

**1.2** "Authorized Users" means Customer's employees, contractors, and agents who are authorized by Customer to access and use the Service under the rights granted to Customer pursuant to this Agreement.

**1.3** "Customer Data" means all electronic data, content, and information submitted by Customer or Authorized Users to the Service, excluding Provider Materials.

**1.4** "Documentation" means Provider's standard user guides, online help, and technical documentation for the Service, as updated from time to time.

**1.5** "Fees" means the subscription fees and any other charges payable by Customer as set forth in the applicable Order Form.

**1.6** "Order Form" means the ordering document executed by the Parties specifying the Service, subscription term, pricing, and other commercial terms.

**1.7** "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person, as defined under applicable data protection laws.

**1.8** "Provider Materials" means the Service, Documentation, and any software, tools, APIs, templates, or other materials provided by Provider, including all improvements, modifications, and derivative works thereof.

**1.9** "Service" means Provider's proprietary software-as-a-service platform identified in the Order Form, including all updates and upgrades made generally available by Provider during the Subscription Term.

**1.10** "Service Level Agreement" or "SLA" means the service availability commitments set forth in Schedule A.

**1.11** "Subscription Term" means the initial subscription period specified in the Order Form and any subsequent renewal periods.

## SECTION 3: SUBSCRIPTION AND PAYMENT

### Option A: Per-User/Seat-Based Pricing

**3.1 Subscription Fees.** Customer shall pay the per-user subscription fees specified in the Order Form for each Authorized User with access to the Service.

**3.2 User Counts.** The number of Authorized Users shall not exceed the quantity specified in the Order Form. Customer may add additional users during the Subscription Term at the then-current per-user rate, prorated for the remainder of the term.

**3.3 Billing.** Provider shall invoice Customer [monthly/annually] in advance. All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

### Option B: Usage-Based Pricing

**3.1 Subscription Fees.** Customer shall pay usage-based fees calculated according to the pricing metrics specified in the Order Form (e.g., API calls, storage, transactions, data processed).

**3.2 Usage Measurement.** Provider shall measure Customer's usage through automated metering systems. Provider's usage records shall be the definitive basis for calculating Fees.

**3.3 Billing.** Provider shall invoice Customer monthly in arrears based on actual usage during the preceding billing period. All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

**3.4 Usage Alerts.** Provider shall notify Customer when usage reaches [75%/90%] of any applicable usage thresholds or spending limits set by Customer.

### Option C: Flat-Rate Pricing

**3.1 Subscription Fees.** Customer shall pay the flat-rate subscription fee specified in the Order Form for unlimited access to the Service within the scope defined therein.

**3.2 Scope Limitations.** Flat-rate access is subject to fair use policies and any scope limitations specified in the Order Form or Documentation.

**3.3 Billing.** Provider shall invoice Customer [monthly/annually] in advance. All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

### Option D: Tiered Pricing

**3.1 Subscription Tier.** Customer has subscribed to the [TIER NAME] tier as specified in the Order Form, which includes the features and limits described in the tier specifications.

**3.2 Tier Upgrades.** Customer may upgrade to a higher tier at any time. Upgraded pricing shall be prorated for the remainder of the current billing period.

**3.3 Tier Downgrades.** Customer may request a downgrade to a lower tier, effective at the next renewal date, subject to migration of Customer Data to comply with lower tier limitations.

**3.4 Billing.** Provider shall invoice Customer [monthly/annually] in advance for the applicable tier. All invoices are due within thirty (30) days of the invoice date.

### Common Payment Terms (All Models)

**3.5 Payment Method.** Customer shall pay all Fees by credit card, ACH, or wire transfer, as agreed by the Parties.

**3.6 Taxes.** Fees are exclusive of all taxes, levies, and duties. Customer is responsible for all applicable taxes, excluding taxes based on Provider's net income.

**3.7 Late Payments.** Overdue amounts shall bear interest at the lesser of 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law. Provider may suspend Service access upon fifteen (15) days' notice of non-payment.

**3.8 Price Changes.** Provider may modify pricing upon at least sixty (60) days' notice prior to any renewal term. Continued use after the renewal date constitutes acceptance of the new pricing.

**3.9 Disputes.** Customer must notify Provider of any billing dispute within thirty (30) days of the invoice date. The Parties shall work in good faith to resolve disputes.

**4.2 Scheduled Maintenance.** Provider shall provide at least forty-eight (48) hours' notice of scheduled maintenance windows. Scheduled maintenance shall not count against SLA calculations.

## SECTION 5: DATA RIGHTS AND SECURITY

**5.1 Customer Data Ownership.** As between the Parties, Customer retains all right, title, and interest in and to Customer Data. Provider acquires no rights to Customer Data except the limited license granted herein.

**5.2 License to Customer Data.** Customer grants Provider a non-exclusive, worldwide license to host, copy, transmit, display, and process Customer Data solely to provide the Service and as otherwise described in this Agreement.

**5.3 Data Security.** Provider shall implement and maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to:
   (a) Protect the security and confidentiality of Customer Data;
   (b) Protect against anticipated threats or hazards to Customer Data security;
   (c) Protect against unauthorized access to or use of Customer Data; and
   (d) Ensure the proper disposal of Customer Data.

**5.4 Security Measures.** Provider's security program shall include:
   (a) Encryption of Customer Data in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher) and at rest (AES-256 or equivalent);
   (b) Access controls limiting personnel access to Customer Data;
   (c) Regular security assessments and penetration testing;
   (d) Incident response procedures; and
   (e) Employee security training.

**5.5 Certifications.** Provider shall maintain [SOC 2 Type II / ISO 27001 / other applicable] certification(s) throughout the Subscription Term and provide evidence of certification upon Customer's reasonable request.

**5.6 Data Processing Agreement.** To the extent Provider processes Personal Data on behalf of Customer, the Parties shall execute a Data Processing Agreement in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

**5.7 Data Location.** Customer Data shall be stored and processed in [REGION/COUNTRIES]. Provider shall not transfer Customer Data outside such locations without Customer's prior written consent.

**5.8 Data Return and Deletion.** Upon termination or expiration of this Agreement, Provider shall:
   (a) Make Customer Data available for export for thirty (30) days; and
   (b) Delete Customer Data within ninety (90) days, except as required by law.

**5.9 Security Incidents.** Provider shall notify Customer within seventy-two (72) hours of discovering any Security Incident affecting Customer Data, and shall provide reasonable cooperation in investigating and remediating such incident.
