| Model | Provider Risk | Client Risk |
|-------|---------------|-------------|

| Joint ownership | Medium | Medium |

- **üî¥ CRITICAL**: Unlimited liability

### Standard Carve-outs from Cap:

- IP infringement
- Gross negligence / willful misconduct
- Personal injury / death

## Quick Risk Assessment by Party

### If You Are the SERVICE PROVIDER, Watch For:
1. Unlimited or excessive liability
2. Background IP assignment
3. Uncapped indemnification
4. Performance guarantees
5. Payment terms >45 days
6. No cure period for breach
7. Non-compete provisions
8. Excessive audit rights

### If You Are the CLIENT, Watch For:
1. No warranties or too narrow
2. Provider keeps all IP
3. Excessive limitation of liability
4. No termination for cause
5. Auto-renewal traps
6. Assignment without consent
7. Inadequate confidentiality
8. No insurance requirements
